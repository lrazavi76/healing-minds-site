About Me

Every journey toward healing begins with a single step — you don’t have to take it alone.

Finding the right support can feel overwhelming, but it can also be the turning point toward hope and change. My role is to walk beside you on that path and help you discover your own capacity for healing.

For more than 20 years I’ve worked in health care, standing with people during some of their most difficult moments. Those experiences have shown me firsthand how powerful empathy, skilled treatment, and steady support can be when someone is struggling. As a board certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP), my deepest commitment is to create a space where you feel safe, respected, and truly heard — a space where you are not defined by a diagnosis but recognized as a whole, unique person.

I take an integrative approach to psychiatric care, looking at the full picture of your life rather than just your symptoms. Together we’ll explore biological, psychological, social, and lifestyle factors that may be shaping your well-being. We will work together to design a treatment plan that fits your unique needs, combining thoughtful medication management with other supportive strategies to help you feel your best.

I value open communication and see our work as a partnership: you bring your story, strengths, challenges, and goals; I bring clinical expertise, compassion, and a non-judgmental perspective.
