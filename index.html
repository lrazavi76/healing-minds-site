Mission

With over 20 years of experience in healthcare, my path began in the NICU, where I learned that healing is not only medical but also emotional. Supporting families during their most fragile moments taught me that the heart of medicine is human connection. That realization stayed with me and ultimately led me to the field of psychiatry.

As a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, my mission is simple yet deeply personal: to offer a place where people feel truly seen, understood, and supported. I believe mental health care should feel like stepping into calm after a storm, where you can speak freely, explore openly, and rebuild with guidance rooted in empathy and evidence-based treatment.

I am committed to helping individuals make sense of their struggles, rediscover their strengths, and move toward a life that feels meaningful and aligned with who they are. My work is not just a profession. It is a continuation of a lifelong calling to stand with people during their hardest moments and help them find their way forward.
