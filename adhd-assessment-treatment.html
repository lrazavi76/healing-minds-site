ADHD Assessment & Treatment

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) can affect focus, organization, motivation, emotional regulation, task completion, and daily functioning. Whether symptoms began in childhood or appeared later in life, many individuals struggle silently, unsure if what they are experiencing is ADHD or something else.

A comprehensive ADHD assessment explores your history, current challenges, strengths, and patterns across home, school, work, and relationships. We also evaluate for conditions that can mimic ADHD, such as anxiety, trauma, depression, or sleep difficulties. This ensures you receive an accurate diagnosis and the most appropriate treatment.

Treatment is individualized and may include medication, behavioral strategies, lifestyle modifications, and supportive tools to improve attention, structure, and emotional balance. Medication can be effective for enhancing focus, reducing impulsivity, improving task follow-through, and supporting daily functioning.

My goal is to help you understand your symptoms clearly, make informed decisions, and build a plan that supports long-term success in a way that feels empowering and manageable.
