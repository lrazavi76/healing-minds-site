Trauma & PTSD Care

Trauma affects the mind and body in powerful ways. Whether the experience was recent or occurred many years ago, its impact can show up through intrusive memories, hypervigilance, emotional numbness, sleep problems, irritability, or a constant sense of fear or overwhelm.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and trauma-related symptoms are not signs of weakness — they are the mind’s protective response to overwhelming experiences. Healing begins with understanding these symptoms and gently working through them at a pace that feels safe.

Trauma-informed psychiatric care focuses on compassion, safety, and collaboration. We explore your symptoms, triggers, and what helps you feel grounded. Medication may be helpful for reducing anxiety, improving sleep, stabilizing mood, or reducing hyperarousal. When appropriate, I also provide referrals to trauma-focused therapy modalities such as EMDR, CBT, or somatic therapies.

My goal is to create a space where you feel supported, validated, and never rushed. You are not alone — recovery is possible, and together we can work toward restoring your sense of safety, strength, and emotional balance.
