Anxiety & Depression Treatment

Anxiety and depression are among the most common mental health conditions—and they are also highly treatable. Each person experiences them differently, so treatment must be personalized and approached with compassion, understanding, and clinical expertise.

Anxiety may involve excessive worry, racing thoughts, restlessness, physical tension, or panic attacks. Depression can include persistent sadness, loss of interest, low energy, changes in sleep or appetite, and difficulty concentrating. These conditions can impact daily functioning, relationships, and overall quality of life.

My role is to help you understand what you are experiencing and provide evidence-based strategies to support relief and long-term healing. Treatment may include medication, lifestyle modifications, coping strategies, and referrals for psychotherapy when needed.

Together, we will create a treatment plan that feels safe, collaborative, and aligned with your goals—moving at a pace that supports your comfort and well-being.
