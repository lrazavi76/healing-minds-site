Medication Management

Medication management is a thoughtful, collaborative process that ensures you receive the right medication, at the right dose, and with the right ongoing support. It’s not simply about prescribing — it’s about understanding your unique needs and monitoring how you respond over time.

During medication management visits, we discuss your symptoms, progress, concerns, side effects, and goals. I provide education on how medications work, what to expect, and how to safely use them. Your comfort and understanding are essential every step of the way.

Medication may help with conditions such as depression, anxiety, ADHD, PTSD, bipolar disorder, OCD, and sleep-related challenges. For some individuals, medication alone is helpful; for others, it works best alongside therapy, lifestyle changes, or additional supports.

My approach is conservative, evidence-based, and always centered around your well-being. We move at a pace that feels right for you, adjusting treatment as needed to help you achieve meaningful and sustainable improvement.
