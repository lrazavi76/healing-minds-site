Self-Pay Rates

For clients who prefer not to use insurance or whose plans do not cover services, self-pay options are available. Self-pay visits may offer:

  • More privacy
  • Flexible scheduling
  • No formal diagnosis required for reimbursement

Please contact me directly for current rates.

Superbills

If your insurance plan offers out-of-network reimbursement, I can provide a superbill upon request. This allows you to submit for potential reimbursement depending on your individual plan. Reimbursement is not guaranteed and varies by insurer.

Questions?

I’m always here to help you understand your coverage and determine whether your insurance may be used for care. Please feel free to reach out anytime.
